Dante D. Sánchez-Gallegos

Hi! I'm Dante D. Sanchez-Gallegos. I'm currently a research assistant at the Charles III of Madrid University. I completed my bachelor in IT engineering at the Polytechnic University of Victoria, Tamaulipas in 2016. I completed a Master degree in Sciences on Engineering and Computational Technologies at the Cinvestav Tamaulipas, Mexico in 2019. In 2023, I received the PhD degree from the Cinvestav Tamaulipas, Mexico.

What I Do

Currently, I'm interested in researching how to improve the design and deployment of scientific and data science systems. I'm also interested in the design and implementation of distributed systems, cloud computing, and processing workflows. My reserach is focused, but not limited, on the following topics:

Cloud and computing continuum

Data management systems for data science

Serverless computing

Data distribution and storage techniques
